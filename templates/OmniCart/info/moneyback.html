Money Back Policy

Return within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.

To initiate the return process:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to "Order History."
  3. Find the order and click "Return."
  4. Follow on-screen instructions.

For assistance, contact customer support.

Additional Options:

  • Extended Return Period: Premium members get 60 days.
  • No Restocking Fees: We don't charge restocking fees.
  • Fast Refunds: Processed within 5 business days.
